🔄 第一部分：开篇改写与同义替换 (Introduction & Paraphrasing)

✍️题干改写核心动词

拒绝重复 show，使用更学术的表达：

  • illustrates (阐明/图示)
  • describes (描述)
  • delineates (描绘/勾勒)
  • presents (呈现)
  • depicts (刻画)
  • compares (比较)

🔄高频名词替换

针对常见图表主题：

  • 数量/货物： quantities of goods → volume of cargo / amount of goods transported
  • 运输方式： modes of transport → means of transport / distinct categories
  • 人均： per person → per capita
  • 排放： emissions → carbon output / release of carbon dioxide
  • 运输： transported → carried / conveyed

🕰️时间表达升级

让时间描述不再单调：

  • between [Year] and [Year] → over a [X]-year period
  • → over four decades (超过四十年)
  • → from... to...
  • → during the period spanning from... to...
🗣️ "The line graph illustrates changes in carbon output over a 20-year period."
⏳ 第二部分：时间轴与时态逻辑 (Time Axis & Tense Logic)

🎯时态判定法则 (核心考点)

过去 纯历史数据： 全程一般过去时 (v-ed)

混合 一过去一将来： 过去时 + 预估词组 (严禁用 will)

未来 纯预测数据： 全程使用预估词组

✅ The figure was 10 in 2000.
✅ It is expected to reach 20 by 2030.
❌ It will reach 20. (不推荐)

🔮预测专用词组

用于将来时间点或预测线：

  • be expected to (被预计会)
  • be projected to (被预测会)
  • be predicted to (被预言会)
  • be forecasted to (被预报会)
  • be likely to (很可能)

📅关键时间介词

  • over a [X]-year period (在...年期间)
  • over two decades (在二十年间)
  • in the first/initial ten years (在最初十年)
  • in the next ten years (在随后的十年)
  • by [Year] (截止到...年)
  • throughout the period (在整个期间)
📈 第三部分：动态趋势描述 (Dynamic Trends)

📈上升趋势 (Increase)

普通上升 rise / increase / grow / climb (攀爬)

急剧上升 surge / soar (猛增) / rocket (火箭般上升) / leap (飞跃)

持续上升 experience a continuous rise / see a gradual but consistent increase

📈 "Prices soared during the first quarter."

📉下降趋势 (Decrease)

普通下降 fall / decrease / drop / decline / dip (小幅下跌)

急剧下降 plummet (暴跌) / plunge (投入/猛跌) / dive (跳水) / slump (萧条/暴跌)

持续下降 follow a downward trajectory / witness a slow but steady decline

📉 "The rate plummeted to a record low."

🌊波动与极值 (Fluctuation & Extremes)

波动 fluctuate / have a volatile trend (不稳定趋势)

平稳 remain stable/steady / level off (变平) / plateau (进入高原期/平稳)

极值 peak at (在...达到峰值) / bottom out (触底) / overtake (超越)

🔄 "The data fluctuated wildly before leveling off."

🧮数值变化逻辑 (By vs To)

这是最容易扣分的地方，必须分清：

BY 差值 (涨了多少/跌了多少)
The price rose by 10%. (价格上涨了10个百分点)

TO 终点 (涨到多少/跌到多少)
It climbed to 50%. (价格攀升至50%)

FROM... TO... 起止点
It fell from 80% to 60%.

🏆 第五部分：高阶逻辑与连贯性 (Advanced Logic & Coherence)

🔗对比与转折 (Contrast)

让段落有层次感：

  • while / whereas (然而 - 连接两个分句)
  • by stark contrast / in contrast (形成鲜明对比)
  • on the other hand (另一方面)
  • conversely / instead (相反地)
  • similarly / likewise (同样地)

➕伴随与结果 (Accompaniment)

将短句合并为长难句（高分关键）：

  • With结构： with an increase of 5% (伴随着5%的增长)
  • Reaching结构： reaching nearly 100 units (达到近100单位)
  • Followed by结构： followed by a modest recovery (随后是小幅回升)
  • Which引导： ..., which was followed by a fall.
  • Making结构： making it the highest figure (使其成为最高值)

💎高分短语结构

直接可用的句型组件：

🔗并列逻辑 (And Logic)

基础连词 and / also / or

高频考点 not only...but also... / both...and...

进阶替换

  • as well as / in addition to / along with
  • similarly / likewise
  • neither...nor... (负向并列)

⚡转折逻辑 (But Logic)

强转折 but / oppositely / on the contrary / conversely / yet / nevertheless / nonetheless

对比 by contrast / in contrast / by comparison / on the other hand

让步转折

  • 连词（接句子）： although / even though / even if / while / whereas / though
  • 介词（接名词）： despite / in spite of / regardless of
  • 高阶替换： despite the fact that + 句子 / notwithstanding

🔀因果逻辑 (Because Logic)

表原因

  • because / since / as (连词 + 句子)
  • due to / because of / as a result of (+ 名词/短语)
  • attribute A to B (把A归因于B) / blame A on B (把A归咎于B)
  • stem from / derive from / result from (源于)
  • owe to (归功于)

表结果

  • result in / lead to (导致)
  • contribute to (促成) / give rise to (引起)
  • trigger (引发) / prompt (促使) / cause (导致)
👨‍🏫 教授的最终指令： 雅思小作文的本质是信息筛选与逻辑重组，而非单纯的词汇堆砌。请遵循以下步骤：
1. 审题：确定时态（过去/将来/混合）和核心趋势。
2. 分组：将相似趋势的数据放在一起写，不要按年份流水账。
3. 造句：优先使用本库的 Formula (公式) 和 With/While 结构。
4. 检查：确保 by 和 to 没有用错。
